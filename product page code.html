About BMW X5

BMW X5 car latest update

Price: Prices for the BMW X5 range from Rs 95.20 lakh to Rs 1.08 crore (ex-showroom pan-India).

Variants: BMW offers the X5 in 2 variants: xLine and M Sport.

Seating Capacity: It is available in a 5-seater configuration.

Colours: The X5 SUV comes in 4 exterior shades: Storm Bay Metallic, Space Silver Metallic, Black Sapphire, and M Portimao Blue.

Engine and Transmission: The BMW X5 comes with 2 engine options: a 3-litre turbo petrol engine which now makes 381PS and 520Nm. The diesel, on the other hand, is a 3-litre unit with an output of 285PS and 650Nm. Both of these engines use 48V mild-hybrid tech for increased performance. They are mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission and power is transferred to all four wheels.

Features: Features on board the X5 include two digital displays (14.9-inch for infotainment and 12.3-inch for instrumentation), 4-zone climate control, ventilated and heated front seats, and panoramic sunroof. The SUV also gets powered front seats with memory function for the driver seat, a heads-up display, and connected car tech with digital key.

Safety: In terms of safety, it gets 6 airbags, dynamic stability control (DSC), cornering brake control (CBC), ISOFIX child seat anchorages, and driver attentiveness alert.

Rivals: The facelifted BMW X5 goes up against the Audi Q7, Mercedes-Benz GLE and Volvo XC90.

$63,800

About Mercedes-Benz GLE

Mercedes-Benz GLE car latest update

Latest Update: The facelifted Mercedes-Benz GLE has been launched in India.

Price: It is priced from Rs 96.40 lakh to Rs 1.15 crore (ex-showroom).

Variants: Mercedes-Benz retails it in three variants: GLE 300 d 4MATIC, GLE 450 d 4MATIC, and GLE 450 4MATIC.

Seating Capacity: The updated SUV is available in a 5-seater layout.

Engine and Transmission: Mercedes-Benz is offering the GLE facelift with three engine options: two diesel and one petrol. All of the engine options get all-wheel-drive (AWD), paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Their details are as follows:

2-litre, 4-cylinder diesel: 269PS/550Nm

3-litre, 6-cylinder diesel: 367PS/750Nm

3-litre, 6-cylinder turbo-petrol: 381PS/500Nm

Features: The 2023 Mercedes-Benz GLE comes with dual 12.3-inch displays (one for infotainment and the other for instrumentation), a 4-zone climate control, electrically adjustable front and rear seats with memory function (front seats), a heads-up display, and a 590W 13-speaker Burmester sound system.

Safety: Its safety net includes nine airbags, a 360-degree camera, traction control, park assist, and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) like blind spot monitoring and autonomous emergency braking.

Rivals: The updated Mercedes-Benz GLE SUV rivals the BMW X5, Audi Q7 and Volvo XC90.

Audi Q7 car latest update

About Land Rover Range Rover Velar

Land Rover Range Rover Velar car latest update

Price: The Range Rover Velar is priced at Rs 87.90 lakh (ex-showroom pan-India).

Variant: It is available in a single, fully loaded Dynamic HSE variant.

Colour Options: Range Rover offers the Velar in four exterior colour options: Zadar Grey, Varesine Blue, Fuji White, and Santorini Black.

Seating Capacity: The Range Rover Velar can seat up to five people.

Engine and Transmission: Land Rover offers the Velar SUV in both petrol and diesel engine options: a 2-litre turbo-petrol (250 PS / 365 Nm) and 2-litre diesel (204 PS / 430 Nm). Both engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and come with all-wheel-drive (AWD) as standard.

Other features on board the Velar include 4-zone climate control, a panoramic glass roof, wireless phone charging, a 1,300W Meridian sound system, air purifier, and 20-way heated, cooled, and massaging front seats.

Safety: Passenger safety is taken care of by multiple airbags, a 360-degree camera, electronic stability control (ESC), park assist, a tyre pressure monitoring system (TPMS), and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS).

Rivals: The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Velar competes with the Mercedes-Benz GLE, Audi Q7, BMW X5, and Volvo XC90.

About Lamborghini Revuelto

Lamborghini Revuelto car latest update

Latest Update: The Lamborghini Revuelto has been launched in India.

Price: It is priced at Rs 8.89 crore (ex-showroom pan India).

Variant: The Revuelto is being offered in a single fully loaded variant.

Engine & Transmission: The hypercar uses a 6.5-litre naturally aspirated V12 petrol engine, mated to a 3-motor setup with a combined power output of 1,015 PS. The power is delivered to all four wheels via an 8-speed dual clutch transmission (DCT). The Revuelto can sprint from nought to 100 kmph in 2.5 seconds.

Features: Lamborghini has equipped the Revuelto with a triple screen setup: a 12.3-inch digital driver’s display, 8.4-inch vertical infotainment display, and 9.1-inch passenger display.

Safety: In terms of safety, the hypercar is equipped with a full suite of advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) like adaptive cruise control, lane change and departure warnings, and rear cross traffic alert. Rivals: The Lamborghini Revuelto takes on the Ferrari SF90 Stradale.
